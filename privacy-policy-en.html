1. Introduction

At Mindstep (“App”), we take your privacy seriously. This privacy policy explains what personal data we collect through the App, how we use it, store it, and protect it.

2. Data We Collect

The following personal data are collected within the App:

  • First Name
  • Last Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Gender
  • Email Address
  • Step counter data
  • Crash and error reports related to app usage (via Crashlytics)

3. Purpose and Use of Data

The collected data is used for the following purposes:

  • Personalizing your app experience
  • Performing gender- and age-based analyses for calorie calculations
  • Sending birthday and motivational notifications
  • Providing weekly analysis reports for premium subscribers
  • Improving app security and performance (error tracking and analysis)
  • Recording and analyzing step counter data

4. Data Storage

All collected data is securely stored on Firebase infrastructure.

5. Data Sharing

Personal data is not shared with third parties. Data is used solely to fulfill the functions of the app described above.

6. User Rights

Under KVKK and GDPR, users have the rights to:

  • Access their personal data,
  • Request corrections to their data,
  • Request deletion of their data,
  • Object to the processing of their data.

You can exercise these rights via the app or by contacting us at meliksahselvi.engineer@gmail.com.

7. Notifications (Push Notifications)

The app may send:

  • Birthday celebration notifications,
  • Motivational messages,
  • Weekly analysis reports for premium subscribers.

Notification permissions can be managed from your device settings.

8. Health and Activity Data

The app collects step counter data to perform analyses based on your steps and movements. This data is used solely for in-app analyses and is not shared with third parties.

9. Security

Appropriate technical and administrative measures are taken to ensure your data security. Data stored on Firebase is encrypted and transmitted over secure connections.

10. Policy Updates

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Updates will be announced within the app.
